Wellness in service work is an ongoing practice, not a one-time fix. It requires intentional care for the people who dedicate themselves to serving others. Whether you're leading a faith community, coordinating volunteers, or managing a mission-driven organization, supporting the wellbeing of your people is essential to sustaining your shared work.
